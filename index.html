Powerful Pokémon Units

PokePath TD features an arsenal of Pokémon units and abilities to turn the tide of any battle. Each unit provides unique advantages and can be the key to victory when positioned strategically.

Fire Types: High damage output that excels against Grass and Ice enemies, but vulnerable to Water.

Water Types: Balanced defense with splash damage that counters Fire and Ground enemies effectively.

Grass Types: Area control units that can slow enemies and deal damage over time.

Electric Types: Fast attack speed with chain lightning that hits multiple targets.

Ground Types: Heavy hitters with stun capabilities that excel against Electric and Rock enemies.

Flying Types: Aerial units that can bypass ground obstacles and attack from above.

Legendary Pokémon: The most powerful units, combining multiple effects for battle-changing moments.
